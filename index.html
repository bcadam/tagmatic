Protect your Identity

Everytime you search, shop, or share online, your activities are tracked. Using your email address, advertisers are able to know your age, your income, and many other data points you'd prefer to keep private.

About EmailBolt

We make it harder for anyone to track your habits online. Use individual email addresses for each newsletter. If this information if ever comprimised, delete the link to your primary email and keep it secure.

EmailBolt provides private disposable email addresses quickly. If one of these email addresses is ever compromised just delete it and keep your primary address secure. Unlike other providers, EmailBolt allows you to own a specific address that cannot be used by others.

Without EmailBolt, every time you use your email address, companies are able to gather information about you e.g. your income, marital status, age, etc. EmailBolt prevents this data from being gathered without your consent.

FAQs

Are these private?

Yes. No one can use the same email bolt as you. Unlike other mail providers that allow mail to be sent to public folders that are accessible to anyone, we value your privacy and want to keep your mail away from prying eyes.

Do you gather information about me?

Never. That's not what we do for kicks. There is a ton of information out there tied to your email address. Everytime you use your address to sign up for a mailing list, a website, or post it in a profile, companies are gathering and selling that to the highest bidder.

Do you have access to my bolted emails?

Never. The bolts that forward to your permanent email are never stored. The email hits the redirect and goes directly to you without us touching, or looking at it.
